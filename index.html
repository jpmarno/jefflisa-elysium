The Property

A Setting Unlike Any Other

Situated within the storied 860-acre grounds of the YMCA of the Rockies in Estes Park, Colorado, this private home occupies one of the most extraordinary addresses in the American West. With Rocky Mountain National Park at your doorstep, you are surrounded by wilderness, wildlife, and a quiet that only the high country can offer.

The YMCA of the Rockies has been a cherished mountain retreat since 1908. To hold a home within these grounds is to be part of a living legacy — a community of families, nature, and the enduring spirit of the Rockies.

This is not a typical mountain cabin. It is a place of genuine rarity: private, serene, and woven into one of Colorado's most beloved landscapes.

Location

Estes Park, Colorado

The YMCA of the Rockies sits at 8,000 feet elevation, just two miles from the entrance to Rocky Mountain National Park and a short drive from the charming town of Estes Park. Denver is approximately 75 miles to the south.

The campus itself offers swimming, tennis, hiking trails, a chapel, dining, and a deep sense of community that has welcomed generations of families since 1908.
